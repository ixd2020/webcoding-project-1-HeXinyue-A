Mr Doodle presents an exhibition at Sotheby’s Hong Kong featuring iconic works reimagined from famous artworks.
He recreated them in very abstract ways and some in a very clear reference to the original.
Here will show off 8 out of the 52 artworks that was at the exhibition.
He has many more amazing artwork, do check out more on his Instagram!
