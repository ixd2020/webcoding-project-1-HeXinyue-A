Art style

A lot of art is lacking a sense of humor. It’s a fun thing to be able to draw and create your own world, and sometimes it is funny. I’m not afraid of people laughing at me or if they don’t get it.

Mr Doodle has built his own distinct style. His work appears on a variety of mediums. It ranges from clothes to walls to anything else he gets his hands on and they are often in thick black line work on white backgrounds. Featuring a wide range of characters, concepts, patterns and linework which group together in a formation, it appears to continue to grow relentlessly. Above all though, his work is about having fun, and not taking things too seriously.

Commercial Brands

Mr Doodle’s doodling has attracted big brands and his growing list of clients includes Adidas, Cass Art, MTV, and many others. He is working for people who are clearly enamored with what he calls his "growing drawing virus". Do read more about the details of the three shortlisted brands Puma, MTV and Samsung that Mr Doodle had worked with before.

Mr Doodle’s online store

He runs his own online store which sells a variety of merchandise featuring his own designs on prints, shirts, original art, and coloring books.
